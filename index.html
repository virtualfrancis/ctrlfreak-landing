Features

Everything in one place

No browser, no VPN to a web UI, no switching between apps. Everything you need to monitor and control your lab is one tap away.

📊
Dashboard
Node-level CPU and RAM utilization, VM and container counts, and a live down-alerts section. Pull to refresh or let notifications find you first.
⚙️
Virtual Machines
All Proxmox VMs and LXC containers across every node. Start, reboot, and stop with confirmation prompts. Backup status visible at a glance.
🐳
Docker Containers
All containers from your Portainer instance in one list. Start, restart, stop, and recreate with a tap. Live log viewer built in.
🟢
Services
Uptime Kuma monitor status pulled directly from your self-hosted instance. See what's degraded or down without opening another tab.
🖥️
Server Hardware
Out-of-band management for bare metal servers. Power state, health status, live temperature sensors, fan speeds, and full power control.
💻
SSH Terminal
Tap into any machine directly from the app. Full interactive shell without reaching for a laptop.
🛡️
Pi-hole
See your query counts, block rate, and blocklist size at a glance. Enable or disable blocking without opening the Pi-hole web UI.
🔒
Tailscale
See which devices on your tailnet are online, their IPs, and last seen times. Know your VPN is healthy before you need it.
🗃️
Unraid
NAS storage array status at a glance. See disk health, array state, and share utilization without opening another browser tab.
🌐
Nginx Proxy Manager
See all your reverse proxy hosts in one view. Active proxies, SSL status, and disabled entries without touching the web UI.
🔧
Network Tools
Ping hosts, check port reachability, test DNS, scan for HTTPS health across your stack, and wake sleeping machines with a tap.

Privacy and Security

No cloud. No middleman.

Your infrastructure data goes from your servers to your phone and nowhere else.

🔐
Credentials in the secure enclave
All server credentials are stored in the OS keychain and encrypted at rest. They never leave your device.
🚫
No backend
There is no developer server, no cloud relay, no analytics, no telemetry. The app connects directly to your infrastructure.
📡
Direct connections only
Every request goes straight from your phone to your servers using credentials you control.
