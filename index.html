No browser, no switching between apps. Everything you need to monitor and control your lab is one tap away.

Dashboard
Node-level CPU and RAM, VM and container counts, and a live down-alerts section. See what needs attention the moment you open the app. Pull to refresh or let notifications find you first.
Virtual Machines & Containers
All Proxmox VMs, LXC containers, and Docker stacks across every node. Start, stop, reboot, and recreate with confirmation. Live log viewer built in.
Server Hardware
Live temperature sensors, fan speeds, and power state for Dell iDRAC and HP iLO servers. Remote power control from your phone.
SSH Terminal
Full interactive shell into any machine in your lab. No laptop, no separate app. Just tap and type.
DNS & Firewall
Pi-hole and AdGuard query stats and blocking controls in one view. OPNsense interface status and gateway health alongside. Manage your network layer from your phone.
Storage & Backups
Array state, volume usage, disk health, and SMART status across Unraid, TrueNAS, and Synology. Proxmox Backup Server task history and job status in the same screen.
Monitoring
Uptime Kuma service status, Beszel agent metrics, and Tailscale device health. Know what's degraded before a user notices.
Network Tools
Ping, port checks, DNS resolution tests, SSL certificate scans, and Wake-on-LAN. Know what's reachable before you SSH in.

Privacy and Security

No cloud. No middleman.

Your infrastructure data goes from your servers to your phone and nowhere else.

🔐
Credentials in the secure enclave
All server credentials are stored in the OS keychain and encrypted at rest. They never leave your device.
🚫
No backend
There is no developer server, no cloud relay, no analytics, no telemetry. The app connects directly to your infrastructure.
📡
Direct connections only
Every request goes straight from your phone to your servers using credentials you control.
🔒
Built for self-signed certs
Works with self-signed certificates out of the box. Homelab servers use them and CtrlFreak accepts them by default. All connections are encrypted end-to-end.
